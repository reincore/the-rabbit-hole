What Is This?

The Rabbit Hole takes something you're curious about and connects it to completely unexpected fields you've never explored.

Type in any topic, interest, or idea. The weirder and more specific, the better.

The Three Modes

  • Standard Descent You enter a topic — the rabbit hole finds surprising connections to it from completely different fields.
  • Deep Wilderness Prepare to go far. Results come from domains so unrelated you'd never find them on your own.
  • Collision Enter two unrelated ideas separated by a slash (e.g., music / botany) and discover what lives at their hidden intersection.
