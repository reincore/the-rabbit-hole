What Is This?

The Rabbit Hole takes something you're curious about and connects it to completely unexpected fields you've never explored. Think of it as a curiosity engine — it breaks you out of your usual reading patterns and shows you surprising connections.

Type in any topic, interest, or idea. The weirder and more specific, the better.

⚡

The Three Modes

  • Standard Engine Finds surprising connections to your topic from other fields.
  • Deep Wilderness Goes wild — results will be from completely unrelated domains. For maximum surprise.
  • Collision Mashes two or more topics together and shows you what exists at that weird intersection.
